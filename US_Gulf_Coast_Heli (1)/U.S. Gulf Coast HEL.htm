Description:
Abstract:
The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart is designed to meet the needs of users who require georeferenced raster images of FAA Visual Flight Rules (VFR) U.S. Gulf Coast Chart. U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart is a raster image of an FAA U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Chart . All information that is part of the paper U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Chart is included in the raster image. The image inside the neat line is georeferenced to the surface of the earth. The raster data may be viewed using a geographical information system to take advantage of the georeferenced nature of the data or the data can be viewed using commonly available image viewing software.
Purpose:
The U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart has been developed to meet the needs of many users. The data has the flexibility of being useful for both industry and individuals. Additional digital data may easily be overlaid on the raster image. Data such as weather, temporary flight restrictions, obstacles, or other geospatial data can be combined with the U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart data to support a variety of needs.
Supplemental_Information:
The U.S. Gulf Coast VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart is provided as one file. The file is 300 dots per inch and 8-bit color. The data is provided as a GeoTIFF.
